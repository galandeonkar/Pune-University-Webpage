About Us

Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU), formerly the University of Poona, is a collegiate public state university located in the city of Pune, India. It was established in 1949, and is spread over a 411 acres (1.66 km ) campus in the neighbourhood of Ganeshkhind.
The university houses 46 academic departments.
